Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1957

The Church has been practising religion with a holy Priest running the Church. There are regularly masses for students and there are regular Sunday mass that anyone can attend.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The library is always packed full of children curious of reading. The library is a great learning place for all, with books about everything you need to know.



School Audiotorium

Was built in 1982

The school audiotorium is a massive place. The whole school can fit in. This audiotorium has a great black stage where students perform their wonderful talents.
